Our Pastor

Pastor Rick Bruschi

Serving in virtually every area of the ministry at Freedom Road Bible Church since its birth in 1991, Pastor Rick was called by God in January of 2001 to the pastorate where he served here at FRBC as Assistant Pastor under his father, Pastor Rick, Sr. In November 2003, Pastor Rick, Sr. went home to be with the Lord and Rick was voted in as Senior Pastor in January 2004. Rick was saved by the grace of God in 1985 and began faithfully serving Him in 2001. He married his high-school sweetheart, Tracy, in August of 1995 and God has blessed them since with three wonderful children: Kaelyn, Ricky and Connor. A graduate of Marist College (BS Business Management, 1995) and Liberty University (MA Biblical Studies, 2011; Mastor of Divinity, 2014), Pastor Rick has a burning desire to follow Christ, make Him known to the world and train and equip the saints to glorify God. Pastor Rick faithfully models our mission of "Reaching, teaching, and leading others so that none would be lost" to the believers at Freedom Road.
