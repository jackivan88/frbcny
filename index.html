Making Jesus Christ known both in our community and around the globe while simultaneously equipping the saints for Christian service is the driving force of all that we do at Freedom Road Bible Church. We offer several areas of ministry geared toward all age groups, from the very young to the most mature adult. We invite you and your family to come and worship the Lord Jesus Christ with our family. We promise that you'll feel right at home!
